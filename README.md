## About

This blog documents my ongoing thinking on how complex social phenomena — opinion polarization, norm diffusion, migration dynamics, misinformation spread — can be understood through the lens of computational modeling, particularly agent-based modeling (ABM).

I write from two angles:

- **As a social observer**: how do real-world social patterns emerge, persist, and change?
- **As a modeler**: how can simulation methods help us formalize, test, and communicate theories about those patterns?
